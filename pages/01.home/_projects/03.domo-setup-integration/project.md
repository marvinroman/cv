title: "DOMO Setup & Integration"
---
#### Problems
* Company wanted more in-depth business intelligence statistics compiled and delivered, daily, weekly, monthly via email.
* Executives wanted less standard reports and more visual reports to better show trends and visual representations of CRM, Accounting & Project data.
#### Solution
* Integrated DOMO with Netsuite.
* Setup fetching of Go Solar California Statistics and pushing into DOMO via SFTP.
* Built out 40+ business intelligence graphs to be sent out to department stakeholders to provide them with internal and external metrics.
