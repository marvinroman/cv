I moved back from Taiwan to take this position with the mandate to re-structure the companies IT & Software footprint to make sure it could handle a company that at the time had just gone through tremendous growth. 
##### Accomplishments
These are of course not not all my individual accomplishments but rather team accomplishments.
* Migrated company from traditional PBX system to VOIP for easier scaling of systems.
* Migrated mail systems to improve spam filtering, security & access.
* Migrated company data & processes from disparate systems, excel sheets, onto central CRM/ERP/Accounting system with software integrations to make a more unified ecosystem.
* Created over 30 roles within the system with separate ACL's & dashboards.
* Increased security footprint by:
    * Making 2nd factor mandatory on all company platforms that offered it.
    * Increased company password hiegene through training, phishing testing & mandated use of a password manager.
    * Moved to integrated firewall & next generation malware protection that talked to each other.
    * Moved offices from flat network to segmented network to both prioritize critical services and protect critical infrastructure.
* Mitigated business interuption by:
    * Creating redundant internet connections with separate backbones.
    * Increasing data retention from local 4 months to indefinate remote cold storage.
* Moved mobile device deployments from manual setup to being pre-deployed from the vendor to our own MDM with preset apps and profiles based on role.
* Moved security camera system from externally managed CCTV to internally managed IP cameras.
* Moved WiFi from individually managed AP's to a unified & centrally controlled AP's.
* Created automated emails & SMS messages to customers as their projects moved through the process to make sure they are kept in the loop on the progress of their energy system installation.
* Created hundreds of reports and working lists within our system to make sure customers flowed smoothly from department to department and nobody got lost between.
* Created custom KPI's within Netsuite to display real-time trends.
* Used SQL formulas in the searches to create advances reporting capabilities across differenct metrics.
* Created external dasboards for executives that gathered data from Netsuite's API and displayed it in graphic visualizations. (see **Business Intelligence Dashboards** Project).
* Migrated all the companies web properties from a central server to a Kubernetes cluster. (see **Kubernetes Cluster** Project).
* Migrated digital advertising from opaque third parties to our own accounts to be managed transparently.
* Created in depth integration between our website forms, our CRM & Google Analytics so we know in detail where the companies sales were coming from.
* Created an company API to sit in the middle of our website & our CRM since we were running into rejections due to concurrency limits and third parties not willing to make SOAP calls with OAuth authentication. (see **Company API** Project).
