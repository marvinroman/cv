#### Problem
* Companies data was held in disparate systems that did not communicate.
* Was using paper, Excel & Google Sheets to track customers.
* Little to no automation and data reporting.

#### Solution
* Vetted ERP, CRM, Accounting software platforms.
* Migrated records from Google Sheets, Project Database & Quickbooks. Correlated records & transactions using PHP, regex, multi-level matching and imported into Netsuite through API.
* Built out record structures, workflows & system configuration.
* Trained 60+ staff members in 6 different departments on their processes in the system.
* Full implementation from contract to go live for the company happened in 6 months.

#### Skills used
 ERP / CRM Software, Data Migration, Regex, MySQL, PGP, Netsuite Administration, Netsuite Development
