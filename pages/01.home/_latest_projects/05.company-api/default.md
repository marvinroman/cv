#### Workflow
* Rebuilt record structure to allow for detailed recording of a solar companies properties, energy systems & customer utility data and their interactions with standard records. 
* Built on the idea of enter once and use in multiple places. Every part of the record structure was built on one to many and sometimes many to many relationships with syncing between records and roll-ups of child record summaries onto parent records and auto calculation of complex system sizing of systems with different inverter types.

#### Skills used
ERP / CRM Software, API, ES6, Netsuite Administration, Suitescript, Javascript, Netsuite Development
